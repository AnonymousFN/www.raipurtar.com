Raipur Tar Products (RTP) established in 1996. The company belongs to the Dhadiwal business group which is one of the oldest business family of Raipur region known for its ethical and reliable business practices. Soon after its inception RTP became one of the leading suppliers to blue chip companies such as ONGC, Assam Gas Company and lndian Oil for its Coal tar based anticorrosive pipeline coating products. Later it .diversified into manufacturing Coal tar Pitch for the Refractory industry and most recently it has commenced manufacture of blast furnace refractory products. The company has a successful track record of manufacturing and supplying over 30000 MT of materials in the last decade to corporate customers.
